The 16th annual KIDSPORTS Invitational Powered by the Warsaw Sports Marketing Center is a nonprofit 3-on-3 basketball tournament for 5th-8th grade boys and girls held at University of Oregon’s Matthew Knight Arena on Sunday, April 12th. Registration is currently open and accessible at www.kidsports.org/warsaw however spots are limited.

The annual tournament is an event consisting of 80 teams, roughly 400 players, that costs only $15 for a team of up to 5 to register. The KIDSPORTS Invitational provides kids with the unique opportunity for kids to play at one of the premier venues in the country, Matthew Knight Arena, have an all-day fun environment, and interact with University of Oregon student athletes regardless of their financial background. The event is free for all to attend, all community members are encouraged to attend.

The tournament began in 1998 as the James H. Warsaw 3-on-3 Classic. Now in its 16th annual iteration, the KIDSPORTS Invitational is organized, promoted, and executed by student members of the University of Oregon’s Warsaw Sports Business Club. The KIDSPORTS Invitational provides Warsaw Sports Business Club members with valuable experiential learning opportunities in event operations, sponsorship, digital media and communications, and creative.

The fundraising effort will seek to strengthen the KIDSPORTS Emerald Scholarship Fund and the Stephen Dember Memorial Scholarship Fund. The goal is to create a life changing event filled with unique opportunities that is accessible to all regardless of financial restrictions.
