Participation is open to ALL 5th-8th grade boys and girls, it is not exclusively for KIDSPORTS players.

The 2015 KIDSPORTS Invitational Powered by the Warsaw Sports Marketing Center is a nonprofit 3-on-3 basketball tournament for 5th-8th grade boys and girls held at University of Oregon’s Matthew Knight Arena on Sunday, April 12th. Registration is currently open and accessible at www.kidsports.org/warsaw however spots are limited. A total of 80 teams will be split up by grade and gender into 8 separate double elimination brackets. Each team will play 1 warm-up game prior to their first tournament game so that each team will be guaranteed at least three games. Along with the tournament there will also be a skills clinic portion of the day put on by University of Oregon student athletes, a dunk contest put on by University of Oregon student athletes, a three point contest, a skills challenge, a free throw contest, and other activities open to all spectators.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ):

Q: What time does the event start?
A: Registration will open at 9:00 am, the national anthem will be performed at 9:30 am, and the first round of warm-up games will begin at 9:40 am.

Q: My kid has never played for KIDSPORTS can he/she still register?
A: Yes, participation is open to all 5th-8th grade boys and girls, it is not exclusively for KIDSPORTS players.

Q: Do spectators have to pay for entrance?
A: No, the event is free for all to attend.

Q: Do teams need to provide their own jerseys?
A: The tournament shirts given to the players will all be the same color. Teams are welcome to use these as their game shirts if they choose. If teams would like to wear a certain color or jersey, they should bring this to the tournament.

Q: How much does it cost to register a team?
A: Team registration costs $15 per team. Each team must have at least 3 participants and a max of 5.

Q: How long is each game?
A: Each game will be 15 minutes long. Every team will be guaranteed at least three games.

Q: Our team doesn't have a coach can we still participate?
A: Yes, while we recommend each team have a coach or parent to manage the team it is not required.

Q: Will there be food provided?
A: Yes, there will be catered food available free of charge for all participants. We're also partnering with local restaurants to provide discounted food both inside the venue and nearby.

The following rules apply to the 2015 KIDSPORTS Invitational:

  • 15 Minute Running Clocks
  • Half Court
  • Scoring by 1's and 2's
  • All shooting fouls = 1 free throw (unless fouled shooting a 2, then 2 free throws)
  • All non-shooting fouls = ball “checked up top”
  • Individual player fouls are not tracked
  • Ball must be “checked” behind the 3-point line after any change of possession
  • If there is a tie at the end of regulation, the game will be played under the “sudden death” format – next basket wins – the ball is “checked up top” by whichever team has the possession arrow
  • Technical fouls may be issued: 1 technical foul = 1 free throw + possession, 2 technical fouls = done for the day
